  • How to file RTI Application with PSP division of the Ministry of External Affairs or the Passport Offices
    Introduction Any citizen of India who desires to obtain any information under the Right to Information Act, 2005 may make a request to the Chief Public Information Officer, PSP Division, Ministry of External Affairs in writing or through electronic means https://rtionline.gov.in. Application Fee In accordance with the directive given in the Gazette notification issued by Department of Personnel & Training Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pension, Government of India vide no. 34012/8(s)/2005 Estt -B dated 16.09.2005 the application for obtaining information under subsection (1) of Section 6 must be accompanied by prescribed application fee. At present the application fee is Rs. 10/-. Mode of payment: For applications submitted in writing at the PSP Division, Patiala House, New Delhi, the Application Fee can be paid in cash or by Demand Draft/ Banker's Cheque/ Indian Postal Order drawn in favour of the Accounts Officer, Ministry of External Affairs, payable at New Delhi. For applications submitted in writing at Regional Passport Offices, the Application Fee can be paid in cash or by Demand Draft/ Banker's Cheque/ Indian Postal Order drawn in favour of the Passport Officer (of the concerned Passport Office), payable at the place where Passport Office is situated. For applications submitted online through the RTI Online Portal (https://rtionline.gov.in), application fee is to be paid online using Internet Banking/Credit/Debit Card/Other payment facilities available on the RTI Online Portal. Persons who belong to BPL category are not required to pay the application fee provided necessary documents are produced in support of their claim.
